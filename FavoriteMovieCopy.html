My Top 5 Most Favorite Movies Of All Time

  1. The Matrix Series

    I just love the highly stylized action sequences and the hidden meanings that threatens the future of humanity

  2. Blade Runner

    I love the cyberpunk aesthetic style. Fun Fact: It was the first movie that introduced the cyberpunk genre and made synthwave music become more popular.

  3. Ghost In The Shell

    Top Notch Quality Animation And The Visuals And Soundtrack Are Mesmerizing.

  4. A Beautiful Life

    The scenes in the movies touch your heartstrings.

  5. Toy Story 3

    Like the story and the character depth and the 3D animation style
