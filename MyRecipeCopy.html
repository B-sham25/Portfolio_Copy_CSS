Fried Noodles Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 carrot cut in strips
  • 100g cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the carrot and cabbage. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add noodles to the other side.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper. Pour the sauce over the noodles and toss to coat.
  5. Continue stir-frying for a few more minutes until the noodles are heated through and well combined with the vegetables and sauce.
  6. Remove from heat and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious homemade fried noodles!
